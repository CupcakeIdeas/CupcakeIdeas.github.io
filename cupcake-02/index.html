Chocolate Fudge Cupcakes

Deep chocolate flavor with a soft crumb and a shiny fudge frosting.

Makes 12 Bake: 17‚Äì19 min Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup (35g) cocoa powder
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup (100g) sugar
  • 1/2 cup (100g) brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) milk
  • 1/3 cup (80ml) neutral oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) hot water or coffee

Fudge frosting

  • 1/2 cup (120ml) heavy cream (or full-fat milk)
  • 1 cup (170g) chocolate chips
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Line a 12-cup pan.
  2. Whisk flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Whisk sugars, egg, milk, oil, and vanilla.
  4. Combine wet + dry just until smooth.
  5. Stir in hot water or coffee. Batter will look thin, that is correct.
  6. Fill liners 2/3 full. Bake 17‚Äì19 minutes. Cool fully.
  7. Frosting: warm cream until steaming, pour over chips + butter + salt. Wait 2 minutes, then stir smooth.
  8. Cool frosting until spreadable. Spoon on or pipe big swoops.
Easy upgrade: Add 1/4 tsp espresso powder to the batter for deeper chocolate.

Decorating ideas

Glossy dip

Dip the cupcake top into slightly warm frosting for a shiny finish.

Cookie crumble

Top with crushed cookies or brownie crumbs for texture.

Chocolate drizzle

Drizzle melted chocolate in zigzags after frosting sets.
