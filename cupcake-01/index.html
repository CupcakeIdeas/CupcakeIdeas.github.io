Vanilla Confetti Cupcakes

Soft vanilla cupcakes with sprinkles and fluffy buttercream. Cute, classic, and beginner friendly.

Makes 12 Bake: 18â€“20 min Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups (160g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup (113g) butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup (150g) sugar
  • 2 eggs, room temp
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) milk
  • 1/3 cup sprinkles (jimmies work best)

Buttercream

  • 1/2 cup (113g) butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1â€“2 tbsp milk
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C). Line a 12-cup muffin pan.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cream butter + sugar until fluffy, 2â€“3 minutes.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, then vanilla.
  5. Mix in dry ingredients and milk, alternating, just until combined.
  6. Fold in sprinkles gently.
  7. Fill liners 2/3 full. Bake 18â€“20 minutes. Cool completely.
  8. Buttercream: beat butter, add sugar, vanilla, salt, then milk to desired fluffiness.
  9. Pipe swirls, add extra sprinkles, chill 10 minutes to set.
Swap ideas: Use almond extract (1/4 tsp) for a bakery vibe, or add lemon zest for brightness.

Decorating ideas

Classic swirl

Use a large star tip. Start on the outside and spiral inward.

Confetti crown

Dip the frosting edge into sprinkles for a neat sprinkle ring.

Mini topper

Add a mini cookie, candy heart, or tiny chocolate square.
