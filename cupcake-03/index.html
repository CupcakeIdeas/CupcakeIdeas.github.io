Strawberry Shortcake Cupcakes

A soft vanilla base with strawberry flavor and a fluffy topping that feels like shortcake.

Makes 12 Bake: 18‚Äì20 min Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups (160g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup (113g) butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup (150g) sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) milk
  • 2 tbsp strawberry jam (or finely chopped strawberries)

Fluffy topping

  • 1/2 cup (113g) butter, softened
  • 1 3/4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tbsp strawberry jam
  • 1‚Äì2 tbsp milk
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Line a 12-cup pan.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cream butter + sugar until fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla.
  5. Mix in dry ingredients and milk, alternating, just until combined.
  6. Swirl in jam gently. Do not overmix.
  7. Bake 18‚Äì20 minutes. Cool fully.
  8. Topping: beat butter, add sugar, jam, salt, then milk to fluff.
  9. Pipe or spoon on, add a berry slice or sprinkle sugar crystals.
Swap ideas: Add lemon zest to the batter, or use raspberry jam for a different fruit vibe.

Decorating ideas

Berry crown

Place a strawberry slice on top and dust lightly with powdered sugar.

Shortcake crunch

Top with crushed vanilla wafers for a shortcake texture.

Two-tone swirl

Pipe vanilla and strawberry frosting together for a striped swirl.
