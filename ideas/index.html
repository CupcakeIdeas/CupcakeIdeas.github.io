Cupcake decorating ideas

A bunch of cute ideas you can do with basic tools. Mix and match and make your own style.

Easy wins

Sprinkle ring

Frost a swirl, then roll the outer edge in sprinkles for a clean halo.

Chocolate drizzle

Use a spoon and let the lines fall naturally. Chill to set fast.

Color dip

Dip the top into glaze. Add one topping only, it looks extra neat.
